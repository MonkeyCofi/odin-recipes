Champorado Recipe


Description:


Champorado is a filipino porridge made out of tableya (a filipino chocolate) and is normally served alongside tuyo (dried fish)


Topped up with milk, and served normally during snacktime, it is a creamy and sweet dish that is enjoyed all throughout the Philippines

Here is a list of the ingredients needed to make this dish:

  • 8 tbsp cocoa powder or about 4 pieces tableya
  • 1 cup glutinous rice malagkit
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups water
  • condensed milk optional
