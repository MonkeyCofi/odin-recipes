Puto Recipe

Description:

Puto is a filipino rice cake, normally made from slightly fermented rice dough.

It is served alongside savoury dishes, which complement each other very well

Here is the list of ingredients needed to make this delicacy:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup fresh milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 5 cups water for steaming
